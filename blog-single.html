OPPO FIND X 2021 - A Gamechanger in Smartphone Design

Samsung invests a significant amount of money each year on advertising campaigns that focus on criticizing Apple's iPhone rather than promoting its own Galaxy phones. However, despite the iPhone being sold for over a decade, Samsung continues to sell tens of millions of phones annually. The real challengers to mobile market sales worldwide are Chinese Android manufacturers like Huawei, Xiaomi, and OnePlus. Oppo, a serious competitor to the top brands in the market, made its largest foray outside of China with the innovative Find X in 2018.

Recently, OPPO launched their latest flagship smartphone, the OPPO FIND X 2. Nearly two years ago, the first model of the FIND X series was released, which transformed the viewpoint and expectations of the majority in the smartphone market. Therefore, this review will concentrate on the inception of the FIND X series.

The FIND X series introduced the first mechanical implementation that achieved an impressive screen-to-body ratio of 93.8%, which revolutionized the smartphone market. This innovation came in response to the release of the iPhone X on November 3rd, 2017, which prompted major brands such as Asus, Xiaomi, LG, Huawei, Motorola, General Mobile, and Samsung to develop designs with reduced black bars at the top and bottom of the screen for a clearer and more user-friendly interface.

“Dare to be different.”

The aforementioned manufacturers were unable to achieve a completely bezel-less design due to the placement of the front-facing camera and sensors. Their solution was to incorporate a small notch at the top of the screen to house the necessary components. Subsequently, a plethora of similar designs have emerged in the market.

The OPPO FIND X introduced the concept of collecting everything inside a notch and hiding it behind a mechanically moving platform, which also served as a design element. This feature was initially viewed as vulnerable due to its newness in design. There were concerns about the protective cases and the durability of the motorized platform.

In addition to its innovative design, the OPPO FIND X also boasted impressive hardware specs. It was powered by the top-of-the-line Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 processor in 2018, with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage. It featured a dual SIM tray, VOOC fast charging technology, a 3730mAh battery, a 20MP+16MP dual camera on the back, and a 25MP front camera. Notably, the device's fast charging technology set a new standard for charging speed and paved the way for even more advanced versions, such as those found in the OPPO FIND X 2.
